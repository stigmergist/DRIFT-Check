# Alignment Matrices — Structured Logic

## Core Rule
Match behaviour to actual state:

| Actual \ Behaviour | Stabilise | Rationalise | Optimise |
|---|---|---|---|
| Stabilise | RIGHT FIT | HIGH RISK | VERY HIGH RISK |
| Rationalise | LIMITED VALUE | RIGHT FIT | HIGH RISK |
| Optimise | LOW VALUE | MISAPPLIED | RIGHT FIT |

## Context Overrides

### Proceed
- Use matrix directly

### Align
- All actions carry alignment risk
- Prioritise agreement before execution

### Probe
- All actions carry uncertainty risk
- Test before scaling

### Stop
- Challenge whether action is needed at all
