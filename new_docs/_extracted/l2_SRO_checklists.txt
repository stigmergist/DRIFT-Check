                                                                      Stabilise - Rationalise - Optimise Checklist
Use this sheet to assess which behaviour a capability most needs right now. Tick each observable item that is clearly present.
Add up the ticks in each column. The highest score usually indicates the dominant need: Stabilise (control and reliability), Rationalise (simplification and waste removal), or Optimise (performance and efficiency improve
Guide only: 0-4 = weak signal, 5-7 = moderate signal, 8+ = strong signal. If two columns are close or tied, treat the result as mixed and use judgement.
